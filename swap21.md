# Privacy Policy for Swap 21

**Last Updated: January 26, 2026**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Swap 21 ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile game.

## Information We Collect

Swap 21 is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

### Data Not Collected

- We do not collect your name, email address, or contact information
- We do not collect your location data
- We do not track your device identifiers for advertising purposes
- We do not collect or store gameplay data on external servers

### Local Data

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

- Game progress (completed levels)
- High scores (best time and lowest shots per level)
- User preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

### Third-Party Services

#### Apple Game Center

If you choose to sign in to Apple Game Center, your Game Center display name and scores may be shared with Apple and visible on leaderboards according to Apple's privacy practices. Please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) for more information.

## Children's Privacy

Swap 21 does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our App.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

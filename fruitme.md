# Privacy Policy

**Fruit Me**
Aksay Games
Last updated: March 2, 2026

## Overview

Fruit Me is an augmented reality game that uses your device's front-facing camera for face tracking gameplay. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the app works.

## Data We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### Camera & Face Tracking
- The front-facing camera is used solely for real-time face tracking during gameplay (detecting mouth position and movement).
- Face tracking data is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded, stored, or sent anywhere.
- Camera footage is only used for optional gameplay video recording, which is saved locally to your device and shared only if you explicitly choose to do so.

### Microphone
- The microphone is used solely to capture audio for optional gameplay video recording.
- Audio is saved locally to your device as part of the gameplay video and is never transmitted to any server.

### Game Center
- If you choose to sign in to Game Center, your scores and achievements are managed by Apple's Game Center service. Please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) for details on how Game Center data is handled.

### In-App Purchases
- Purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

### Local Data
- Game settings, statistics, and high scores are stored locally on your device using standard app storage. This data is not transmitted anywhere.

## Data We Do Not Collect

- No analytics or tracking
- No advertising or ad identifiers
- No location data
- No contacts or address book access
- No third-party SDKs that collect data
- No server-side data storage of any kind

## Children's Privacy

Fruit Me does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app contains no advertising, no social features that allow communication between users, and no data collection.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version here with an updated date.
